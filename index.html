Our mission

Every child deserves a shot at building something of their own.

Smart Pig was founded on a simple belief: when you give a child the chance to run a real business — make real decisions, real mistakes, and real money — you teach them more than any textbook can.

We work with schools, community centres, and families in underserved neighbourhoods to put entrepreneurship within reach of every child, regardless of background.

From bake stalls to T-shirt brands to neighbourhood tutoring services, our young founders are learning to spot problems, design solutions, and believe in their own ideas.
